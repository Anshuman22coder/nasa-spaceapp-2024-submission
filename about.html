About GLOBE Quest

GLOBE Quest is an innovative web-based game that combines education with interactive gameplay, focusing on real-world environmental research. By following GLOBE protocols, students embark on environmental challenges, gather data, and contribute to global research efforts while competing with others. Our goal is to make environmental education fun, competitive, and impactful.

Our mission is to foster environmental awareness and action among students, helping them understand critical issues facing our planet. By participating in GLOBE Quest, students not only become learners but also contributors to scientific discovery.

Technologies Used

GLOBE Quest is built using a variety of modern technologies to ensure a smooth and engaging user experience. Here are some key technologies we utilize:

AWS CloudFront: Used for content delivery, ensuring fast and reliable access to our game resources globally.
AWS Amplify: Facilitates backend services, allowing us to easily manage data, authentication, and hosting.
HTML: The foundational markup language used to create the structure of our web pages.
CSS: Used for styling our web pages, ensuring a visually appealing and responsive design.
JavaScript: Powers interactive features and enhances the user experience within the game.
React Native: Utilized for building a mobile-friendly version of GLOBE Quest, enabling students to play on various devices.
MongoDB: A NoSQL database that stores user data and environmental research findings in a flexible format.
Figma: Used for designing the user interface and creating a seamless user experience throughout the game.
